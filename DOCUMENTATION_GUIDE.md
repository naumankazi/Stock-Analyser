## 💡 Pro Tips

### **Tip 1: Customize Before Sharing**
Don't just copy-paste. For each organization:
- Add their **name** in examples
- Reference **their sector** (banking, insurance, etc.)
- Adjust **ROI numbers** to their team size
- Mention **their competitors** to show competitiveness

### **Tip 2: Start with Executive Summary**
Never lead with the 20-page guide. Start with the 2-pager.
- If they ignore it → they're not interested yet
- If they ask questions → share BUSINESS_OVERVIEW.md
- If they want to use it → share FINANCE_GUIDE.md

### **Tip 3: Use for Training**
Once they buy:
- FINANCE_GUIDE.md becomes your **onboarding Bible**
- Analysts read it → they become power users
- You spend less time training (they already know concepts)

### **Tip 4: Reference in Conversations**
Instead of explaining, say:
- *"See page 7 of BUSINESS_OVERVIEW for the risk management approach"*
- *"FINANCE_GUIDE explains why we use PT1/PT2/PT3 segmentation"*
- *"EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY shows the exact ROI numbers for teams your size"*

## ✅ Pre-Sharing Checklist

Before you send any document:

- [ ] Read it yourself (catch typos, ensure alignment with your verbal pitch)
- [ ] Customize examples (mention their organization, not generic)
- [ ] Update any numbers (ROI, pricing) if changed since original draft
- [ ] Add cover letter (1-2 sentences, personalized)
- [ ] Include contact info + next steps
- [ ] Format for easy reading (PDF, proper margins, readable font size)
- [ ] Test links (if sharing digital copy)
- [ ] Mention timeline ("Looking forward to talking next week")
