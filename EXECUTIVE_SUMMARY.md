# StockAnalyzer — Executive Summary (One-Page Pitch)

## 🎯 The Problem

Investment teams spend excessive time on mechanical screening work:
- **Screening 50 stocks manually** → 4–5 hours
- **Analyzing 1 stock deeply** → 30–45 minutes
- **Building trade plans** → 20 minutes per stock
- **Consolidating reports** → 1–2 hours

**Result:** Analysts spend 70% of time on mechanical work, 30% on high-value research.

---

## ✨ The Solution: StockAnalyzer

A **2-in-1 investment intelligence platform** that automates technical analysis and stock screening:

### **Feature 1: Instant Technical Analysis** (30 seconds)

Enter any stock ticker → Get:
- ✅ 2-year interactive candlestick chart
- ✅ 15+ professional indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ADX, SuperTrend, ATR, etc.)
- ✅ Trend analysis (uptrend / downtrend / consolidation)
- ✅ Support/resistance + Fibonacci levels
- ✅ **Trade levels:** Exact entry zone, 3 profit targets, 3 stop-losses
- ✅ **Confidence score** for each target (in %)
- ✅ **Risk score** (1–10)

**Time Saved:** 30 min/stock → 30 sec/stock (99.7% faster)

---

### **Feature 2: Automated Stock Screener** (30 seconds)

One-click screening of **38 pre-filtered Shariah-compliant Indian stocks**:

- ✅ Composite scoring (0–100) on: valuation, growth, profitability, momentum, cash flow, margins
- ✅ Top 3 conviction picks with reasoning
- ✅ Trade plan for each stock (entry, SL, targets)
- ✅ **Risk-weighted portfolio allocation** (safer stocks get more capital)
- ✅ Market overview + sector trends

**Time Saved:** 4 hrs manual screening → 30 sec automated screening (99.9% faster)
