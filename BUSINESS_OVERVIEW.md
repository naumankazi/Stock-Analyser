## 📋 Quick Overview

StockAnalyzer is a **ready-to-use investment analysis platform** that helps financial professionals make faster, data-driven decisions on stock selection and portfolio construction. It provides two core capabilities:

1. **Deep Technical Analysis** — Evaluate any stock worldwide with professional-grade charts and indicators

**Use Case:** Investment teams, portfolio managers, research analysts, and financial advisors can use this platform to:
- Screen stocks faster than manual research
- Identify entry/exit points with calculated risk levels

## 📊 The 15+ Professional Indicators (Explained for Finance Professionals)

All indicators are calculated automatically. Here's what each one tells you:

| Indicator | What It Reveals | Why It Matters |
|-----------|-----------------|---|
| **Moving Averages (50/100/200-day)** | Trend direction and support zones | Identifies uptrend (price > MA200) vs downtrend (price < MA50) |
| **RSI** | Overbought (>70) / Oversold (<30) conditions | Signals potential reversals or continuation strength |
| **MACD** | Momentum acceleration and slowing | Confirms early-stage trend changes before price moves |
| **Bollinger Bands** | Volatility expansion/contraction | Wide bands = high volatility; narrow = range-bound trading |
| **ADX** | Trend strength on 0–100 scale | ADX > 25 = strong trend exists; ADX < 20 = no trending |
| **SuperTrend** | Dynamic buy/sell signals | Mechanical entry/exit indicator for traders |
| **ATR** | Average daily price movement range in % | Risk gauge: 2% ATR = ±2% daily swings typical |
| **OBV + VWAP** | Institutional buying/selling pressure | Confirms if volume is supporting the price move |
| **Fibonacci Levels** | Key mathematical retracement zones | Predict S/R levels using golden ratio (23.6%, 50%, 78.6%) |
| **Pivot Support/Resistance** | Previous day's key price levels | Short-term traders use for intra-day entry/exit |
| **Chart Patterns** | Double tops, head & shoulders, etc. | Visual confirmation of trend reversals |

**Philosophy:** These indicators combine **trend analysis** (where is the stock going?), **momentum** (is the move accelerating?), **volume** (is buying pressure real?), and **volatility** (how much risk?).

## 🛡️ Risk Management Built In

### **Portfolio-Level Risk Controls:**

1. **Max Risk per Stock** — Prevent over-concentration
   - Set max downside per holding (e.g., "No stock can drop more than 7%")
   
2. **Risk-Weighted Allocation** — Safer stocks get more capital
   - Low-risk stocks (score 80+) → 35% of portfolio
   - Mid-risk stocks (score 60–80) → 45% of portfolio
   - High-risk stocks (score <60) → 20% of portfolio

3. **Position Sizing** — Optimal entry amounts
   - Ensures losers don't wipe out winners
   - Maximizes risk-adjusted returns

### **Trade-Level Risk Controls:**

1. **Stop-Loss Placement** — Limit loss per trade
   - SL1 (nearest): 2–3% below entry
   - SL2 (medium): 4–6% below entry
   - SL3 (far): 8–10% below entry

2. **Profit Target Tiers** — Reduce greed risk
   - PT1 (conservative): 3–5% above entry (easy to hit, high probability)
   - PT2 (aggressive): 8–12% above entry (harder to hit, medium probability)
   - PT3 (max): 15–20% above entry (rare, high conviction only)

3. **Confidence Score** — Know your odds
   - PT1: 75% probability of hitting
   - PT2: 55% probability of hitting
   - PT3: 35% probability of hitting

**Psychology:** By segmenting targets, you avoid the "hold and hope" trap—take profits systematically.
